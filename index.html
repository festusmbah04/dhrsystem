Telehealth Consultations

Access Expert Care from Anywhere

Experience the convenience of virtual healthcare consultations with DHRsystem. Connect with experienced doctors, specialists, and healthcare professionals from the comfort of your home, office, or wherever you are. Whether you need medical advice, diagnosis, or ongoing management of chronic conditions, makes DHRsystem quality healthcare accessible and convenient.

Accessing the Telehealth Platform

Users log in to their DHRsystem accounts via the website or mobile app. They navigate to the "Telehealth" section within the platform.

Booking an Appointment

Users select a preferred date and time for their telehealth consultation from the available slots. They provide any relevant information or notes about their health concerns for the healthcare provider to review.

Confirmation and Reminders

Upon booking, users receive an instant confirmation of their appointment details. DHRsystem sends reminders to users prior to their scheduled appointment time, ensuring they don't miss their consultation.

Secure Video Consultation

At the appointed time, users join the telehealth consultation through the DHRsystem platform. The platform utilizes encrypted communication technology to ensure the privacy and security of the video call. Users are connected with their chosen healthcare provider, who initiates the consultation.

Discussion and Diagnosis

During the consultation, users discuss their health concerns, symptoms, and medical history with the healthcare provider. The provider may ask additional questions, perform virtual examinations, or review any relevant test results uploaded to the platform. Based on the discussion and assessment, the provider offers a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to the user's needs.

Prescription and Follow-Up Care

If necessary, the healthcare provider may prescribe medications, order further tests, or recommend lifestyle modifications. Users have the option to request prescription refills or medication adjustments through the platform. The provider schedules any necessary follow-up appointments or monitoring sessions to track progress and adjust the treatment plan as needed.
